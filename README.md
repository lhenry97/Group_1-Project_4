### Project Proposal

# Goal of the Project:
The purpose is to assist with breast cancer survial through early intervention 
The aim of our project is to use machine learning to predict whether breast cancer is Benign or Malignant based on a cancers visual characteristics. These characteristics include mean radius, mean texture, mean perimeter, mean area, mean smoothness, mean compactness, mean concavity and mean concave points.
